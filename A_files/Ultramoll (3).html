Ultramoll PP

General Information

Synonyms: Phthalic polyester
Chemical Category: Polymeric plasticizers

Usage and Performance Characteristics

Manufacturer: Bayer
Outstanding Properties: Oil, grease & hydrolysis-resistant, low migration, good heat resistance, good electrical properties
Used for Polymeric Materials: PVC, NBR
Used for Products: Printing rolls, coatings, film, cable & wire

Physical Properties

Aggregate State at 25°C: Viscous liquid
Density, kg/m3: 1,053 (0°C ); 1,040 (20°C); 1,035 (25°C); 1,026 (40°C); 1,019 (50°C); 1,011 (60°C)
Dynamic Viscosity, cP: 18,000 (0°C); 2,000 (10°C); 1,000 (25°C); 800 (30°C); 650 (40°C); 80 (70°C)
Pour Point, °C: -25

Chemical Properties

Acid Number, mg KOH/g: 0.5
Saponification Value, mg KOH/g: 310

Optical Properties

Color: Colorless
Color, Platinum-Cobalt Scale: 150
Refractive Index: 1.503 (n20D)

Hazard-Related Data

Flash Point Method: OC
Flash Point Temperature, °C: 230
