Polimix 50

General Information

Synonyms: Polyester based on adipic acid & polyhydric alcohols
Chemical Category: Polymeric plasticizers

Usage and Performance Characteristics

Manufacturer: Lonza Group Ltd.
Outstanding Properties: Resistance to oil & fat extraction, low migration
Used for Polymeric Materials: PVC
Used for Products: Oil resistant cables & hoses, gaskets, tubes

Physical Properties

Aggregate State at 25°C: Liquid
Density, kg/m3: 1,110 (25°C); 1,092 (40°C); 1,070 (60°C)
Dynamic Viscosity, cP: 5,200 (25°C); 1,950 (40°C)

Chemical Properties

Acid Number, mg KOH/g: 2.0 (max)
Odor: Slight

Optical Properties

Color: Pale yellow
Color, Platinum-Cobalt Scale: <150
Refractive Index: 1.467 (n20D)
